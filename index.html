🔍 What Is WebVerse?

WebVerse is the front end of the Web Wide Worlds framework—a tool for jumping between remixable, persistent 3D places using nothing but the web.

🪄 Easy
Build your own world in just minutes and share it with anyone!
🔓 Always Available
Go into Web Wide Worlds anytime! It doesn't matter where you are or on what device.
🕸️ Decentralized
No one owns Web Wide Worlds! You control what you create.

🛠 Technology Stack

WebVerse

The World Browser: Your portal to expansive digital worlds.

WorldHub

All-In-One place to explore, create, and share worlds.

VEML

Simple language for describing worlds: like HTML, but in 3D.

WorldOS

Modular server framework for shared, flexible, and scalable worlds.
